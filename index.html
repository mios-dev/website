DARK SIDE

Let the content play the first fiddle. This will help you focus on what you want to, while all toolbars, menus are just a background. It creates beautiful and distraction-free environment that's good for your eyes and makes you more productive. All the native apps support it and developers are adding support for it too!

SILENT MODE

Remember talking about distraction-free environment in the first section? This is another option which can help with that. Silent mode is a part of MiOS - if you activate - no notification pop-ups will show. Everything will be hidden under notifications icon in the taskbar.
